About Me

It is my calling to collaborate with a team of creatives and help bring amazing content to the world. I enjoy building interactive worlds full of excitement and fantastic stories. I have been writing tabletop campaigns as a hobby and for volunteer work for 7 years. As that passion grew, so did my interest in video games, and my curiosity about UI and UX began. How does one incorporate a narrative or branding into the UI and enhance user experience? With my flexible communicative approach to customizing narratives, I am always ready to incorporate new ideas, build on existing ideas, and offer constructive feedback in the design process to do just that. Mastering empathetic communication is my greatest achievement. Incorporating and teaching those skills allows for free utilization of constructive feedback. Creating that kind of cohesion and understanding among professionals results in a work culture that essentially says, "we're all in this together; let's grow together and create something to be proud of."
